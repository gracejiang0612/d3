Each dot represents one child reported to New York's poison center for cannabis exposure in 2025. Even this number is an undercount, because hospitals are not required to report cases.
Edibles account for more than half of all cases, often because they look like ordinary candy. Unregulated products, which don't follow state packaging rules, are especially dangerous for kids.
Children 5 and under are the most exposed group across every substance category. Among teens, most cases are intentional — which often from not realizing edibles take up to 90 minutes to kick in.
